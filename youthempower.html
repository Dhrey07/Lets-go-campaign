Youth Empowerment

Jobs

At LGC, we believe in empowering youth through meaningful employment opportunities. Our mission is to equip young individuals with the skills, knowledge, and resources they need to thrive in today's competitive job market.



Here's what sets us apart:

1.

Job Placement Assistance: We assist youth in finding suitable job opportunities through our extensive network of partner organizations. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to match candidates with employers, facilitating successful job placements.

2.

Youth-Centric Approach: We understand the unique challenges faced by young people in securing employment. Our programs and initiatives are specifically tailored to address these challenges, providing youth with the support they need to succeed.

3.

Skill Development: We offer a range of training programs and workshops designed to enhance the employability of young individuals. From technical skills to soft skills like communication and leadership, we provide comprehensive training that prepares youth for the workplace.

4.

Industry Partnerships: We collaborate with leading organizations and industry experts to ensure our programs align with current market demands. By connecting youth with potential employers, we create pathways for meaningful employment and career growth.

5.

Mentorship and Guidance: Our experienced mentors provide one-on-one guidance, coaching, and support to help youth navigate their career paths. Through mentorship programs, we foster personal and professional development, unlocking the full potential of young individuals.

Together, we can create a world where every young person has access to fulfilling and sustainable employment. Join our community.


Leadership Training & Basic Skills

We equip young individuals with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to become effective leaders and make a positive impact in their communities, also equipping them with the practical skills they need to thrive in the modern workforce. We understand the importance of vocational education in opening doors to opportunities and enabling sustainable livelihoods. Through our programs and initiatives, we aim to empower youth to become skilled professionals and contribute to the growth of their communities.



Here are some popular basic skills to consider:

1.

Carpentry and Woodworking: Learning carpentry skills equips individuals with the ability to work with wood, construct furniture, and engage in building projects.

2.

Plumbing: Plumbing skills involve the installation, repair, and maintenance of water supply and drainage systems in residential and commercial settings.

3.

Electrician: Electricians specialize in the installation, maintenance, and repair of electrical systems, including wiring, fixtures, and electrical appliances.

4.

Welding: Welding: Welding skills involve joining metal parts together using heat and pressure. This skill is highly valuable in industries such as construction, manufacturing, and automotive.

5.

Automotive Repair: Learning automotive repair skills enables individuals to diagnose and fix mechanical issues in vehicles, providing maintenance and servicing.

6.

Cooking and Culinary Arts: Culinary skills encompass food preparation, cooking techniques, and menu planning, offering opportunities in restaurants, hotels, and catering services.

7.

Hairstyling and Barbering: These skills involve cutting, styling, and grooming hair, providing services in salons, barbershops, and the beauty industry.

8.

Graphic Design: Graphic design skills include creating visual content, layouts, and branding elements using digital tools. This skill is in demand in marketing, advertising, and media sectors.

9.

Tailoring: Tailoring involves the art of creating and altering garments to fit individual measurements. By learning tailoring skills, individuals can master the techniques of pattern making, cutting, sewing, and garment assembly.

10.

Healthcare Assistance: Our experienced mentors provide one-on-one guidance, coaching, and support to help youth navigate their career paths. Through mentorship programs, we foster personal and professional development, unlocking the full potential of young individuals.
