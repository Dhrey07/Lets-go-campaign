Care packages for our Protectors: Street by street (Police, Soldiers, Lastma etc).

Summary

To provide care packages for government security officials who risk their lives, health, sanity and wellbeing to protect every single citizen in Nigeria and are yet still paid meagre sums of money with little or no protection from the government.

The care package for protectors identifies each protector one Nigerian street at a time delivering care packages to each uniformed official to appreciate them, care for them and sure they are well take care off.

Goal

On street at a time starting from Herbert Macaulay Way to every single street in Nigeria that are manned by every security official. Once all that is captured we moved to the next stage.

Who are the security officials?

  • Police men
  • LASTMA and traffic officials
  • Soldiers
  • Navy
  • Every other identified government security official
